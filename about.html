TradeScope
International Market & Economic Intelligence

About this project

TradeScope is an independent economics research project tracking macroeconomic conditions, trade flows, and key indicators across major economies. It is built and maintained as a portfolio project demonstrating applied economic analysis.

What's tracked

For each country, TradeScope covers GDP growth, inflation, unemployment, trade balance, current account position, FDI inflows, top export categories, and primary trade partners. A qualitative economic summary contextualises the data with structural and cyclical factors.

Data sources

Data is drawn from publicly available sources including the World Bank Open Data portal, IMF World Economic Outlook database, and OECD Statistics. Figures are updated periodically and represent approximate annual or most-recent-available values.

Built with

Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Hosted on GitHub Pages. No frameworks, no dependencies — intentionally lightweight so the data stays front and centre.

Contact

This project is maintained by an economics undergraduate at the University of Calgary. For questions or data corrections, reach out via GitHub.
